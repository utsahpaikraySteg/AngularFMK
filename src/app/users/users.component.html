About Culture Booklet

Culture Booklet is a platform that brings you a plethora of diverse culture beautiful and diverse cultures from different corners of the world. Culture Booklet also allows people to share their unique culture by posting pictures or videos and in return it offers $0.4 USD per every 1000 views on the particular post. It is a platform to explore, share, earn & educate people. In short it’s a place to learn & earn.

Getting Started

You are just few steps away from earning $$$ dollars!! It is even simpler than it sounds.

  • Free Sign up at Culture Booklet to begin with your first post.
  • Click on the menu button then click on My Posts.
  • Click on Add Blog on the left navigation menu and make your first post.
  • After you are done with your post, it will be auto saved as a draft.
  • The review team will then review your post and publish it within 24 hours.
  • You will get paid based on Google Analytics view report based on the payment frequency you choose.
  • You can see your views under blog dashboard after you login.

Culture Booklet Publishing Guidelines

To ensure that Culture Booklet viewers are guaranteed an amazing experience, every post is reviewed by our review team before being published. Posts that don’t meet with the review guidelines will not be approved and published. Please go through the guidelines below before uploading your post.

  • Content must be related to culture, etiquette, people, language, region, city, state or country.
  • Original content, images or videos are highly recommended.
  • Inappropriate content, picture, video will not be published and the profile will be banned from attempting to post them.
  • Content encouraging violence, hatred, social difference will not be published.
